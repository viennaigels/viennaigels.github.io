About

Vienna's NEWEST non profit Triathlon club
(Established in January 2015 over a bottle of wine and broken dreams).
We don't care if you ride your grandmothers steel bike with square wheels or if you have an 80,000 Euro carbon fiber jet bike.
We don't care if you're a student or the CEO of an international bamboo basket company.
If you like Triathlon, you're our kind of person!
Tired of clubs where people take themselves too seriously or are so "stressed" it takes the fun out of the sport?
We don't have that problem! We're fun and non judgmental.
We are a friendly, mutually supportive, fun group of people that train and support each other in Triathlon.
There is no other club like this in Vienna Austria.

Perhaps you are looking for someone to train with? Because we are also looking for new members.
So whether you are an accomplished Triathlete or a friendly beginner, we welcome you
(and usually over a glass of a nice white wine).

    Be friendly, open, and “real”
    Be courteous, punctual and never cancel after you commit
    Support one another in words and actions
    We train in the rain-“You can’t pick the weather on race day”
    Have fun
